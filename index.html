Our Story

Bringing Authentic Flavors to Your Table

Lemor Shawarma was born out of a passion for bringing the vibrant, bold flavors of Middle Eastern street food to our community. Every wrap we craft follows traditional recipes passed down through generations.

We source the freshest local produce, use only halal-certified meat, and slow-roast everything on a traditional rotating spit — because great shawarma takes time and love.
